1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality like navigation and session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display relevant advertisements.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary and deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by our website directly.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by our analytics and advertising partners.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Change your browser settings to accept or reject cookies.
  • Delete existing cookies from your browser.
  • Use private browsing mode to prevent cookies from being stored.
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies.
